HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Our story goes back around 20 years.Armed with a passion for coffee, we had a vision to create The Best Brazilian Coffee Shop across Ireland. We started off simply trying to make a few local independent coffee houses work. And they did! We are 100% independent.Since opening we’ve been recognised and won twice in the Westside Restaurant and Bar Awards. Centrally located and inside the stunning surrounding of City Center, we are the perfect escape for those wanting Quality Coffee, signature cakes, lunch or a perfect place to meet up with friends !

QUALITY AND CONSISTENCY

Making great coffee takes some doing. So we partnered with a small Brazilian Farm. We open our Roastery,and we developed special Blend Coffee that is exclusive just to 60MlsCoffee. Regularly we invent new Blends and impress our Coffee Shop Quests with new Coffee Sensations.

Our Roastery

Roasting coffee transforms the chemical and physical properties of green coffee beans into roasted coffee products. The roasting process is what produces the characteristic flavor of coffee by causing the green coffee beans to change in taste.
